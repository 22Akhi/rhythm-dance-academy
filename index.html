BAISHAKHI GANGULI

Baishakhi Gnguli is an accomplished Odissi Dancer, she started her dance career under the expert guidance of Guru Smt. Dona Ganguly. She has started her dance career in 1998 , she has also had the opportunity of seeking guidance from great maestros and dancers like Guru Kelucharan Mohapatra, Guru Ratikant Mohapatra ,Smt. Sujata Mohaptra, Smt Madhavi Mudgal and Smt Aruna Mohanty which has further enriched her approach to the arts.

She has performed in various prestigious programme in all over the world.Her performances include UdayShankar Festival,Doordarshan Kalakendra,EZCC,Maihar Festival,Konark Festival,Sitapur Mahautsav, Shankatmochon festival,Mukteswar,Triveni Mahautsav etc in India. She has also performed in NABC (North American Bengali Conference) in 2007(Detroit,USA) ,London Ananda Utsav, World Expo in Shanghai China etc. She is the winner of Doverlane Music Conference,where she is being chosen the topper in all categories and best qualifying dancer. She has also received Sanjukta Panigrahi Memorial Award in 2009.
She started her dance school "Rhythm Dance Academy", from 2004 and came a long way of 17years till now.
