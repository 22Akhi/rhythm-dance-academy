What we do?

OUR PROGRAMS

ODISSI DANCE

Development Program (11 and Above)

Our educators work closely with students, providing a holistic approach to their training while taping to each individual dancer to grow their knowledge. Through Development program students learn Odissi repertoire which includes Botu, Pallavi, Abhinaya and Mokshya.

Foundation Program (5-10)

Through Foundation Program children are introduced to stretching and warm up exercises and then to some basic steppings of Odissi and Mangalacharan. Through out these classes, children develop movement creativity, fitness and body awareness which helps to build their self -confidence and allows their physical development.

Creative Classes

Students enjoy creative,folk and contemporary dance items. So we provide a class designed only for creative, folk and contemporary dance where dancers use their natural energy to portray personal expression in their dance techniques.

Rabindranritya

We provide classes for rabindrik dance based on odissi steppings, where dancers can express their feelings. The main objective of Rabindra nritya/natya is to express the emotions in the songs, which is why the dance movements so blend with the rhythm and lyrical beauty of the songs.

Achievements

We are motivators, facilitators, peace-makers, organizers, managers, planners, visionaries, and so on. We like to focus on the role of “motivator" and how teachers can motivate students by celebrating their achievements!

Dance Competition

No matter how many awards we have received, our nerves are always overcome by adrenaline and emotion in the minutes that follow as the music starts. Each performance teaches us the importance of team work and we gain and grow through every competition.

Dance Exam

We have facilities to give dance exam under Chandigarh University. We conduct theory classes throughout the year to prepare our students for the exam beforehand. Special classes are also taken as the exam nears and individual care is taken so that every student passes the exam with flying colors.

Basanta Utsav

We celebrate this combined festival of holi andandthe spring season with elegance. Our programme starts with the dance performances by the kids and is followed by the seniors, teachers and sometimes even parents. Odissi, rabindra nritya, and folk dances are performed with grace. We end the day by applying colors to our teachers, elders and friends.

Parent's Participation

Parents participation in making the Institute what it is today is beyond doubt. We have received immense support and appreciation since the beginning. They take part in all the smallest of activities that we organise. Some have even performed in our annual program and basant utsav. This shows that we operate like a family and each of our member counts.

Annual Programme

The most integral part of our Institute's activities is the annual program. It's the day of celebration, felicitation, feast and festivity. We rehearse for the day from months before. The students take part in creating the props, the dresses and other backstage activities.It's a great opportunity to build the confidence of the students on stage .
