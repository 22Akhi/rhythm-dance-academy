Can't make it to Dance Studio?

As we navigate this provincial shutdown together, Rhythm Dance Academy is committed to bringing the joy of dance into your home and in your dancer’s life every single day. Even though our virtual classes look a little bit different than in-person, we’re thrilled by the flexibility of our dancers, teachers, and parents as we tackle this new reality together. Watching the dancers’ faces light up on Google Meet reminds us that dance is so much more than the steps. Our students get so much out of our classes, whether they are in-person or through a screen. Right now, this is clearer than ever!

The world has been slowly trying to adapt to the new normal since COVID-19 made an appearance in our lives. Quarantine has forced us to find new and creative ways to continue doing what we love but from a distance while trying to feel connected.It’s safe to say we are all craving social interaction right now. Getting the chance to see and hear a familiar face is so valuable right now.
