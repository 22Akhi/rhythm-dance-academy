REGULAR CLASS

One of the most vital aspects of Rhythm's functions is Odissi dance training. The course for regular classes in Odissi dance has been designed mainly for residents of Kolkata, and peripheral areas who can benefit from classes every week, there are also creative classes for them. We have two Programmes based on Odissi Dance for Juniors and Seniors.The Foundation Program is for 5-10years, and Development Program is for 10 and Above.

SPECIAL CLASS/WORKSHOP

A learner can enroll for a minimum of the course period of seven days (at any time of the year) or can enroll for a longer period, depending on individual needs and convenience.
