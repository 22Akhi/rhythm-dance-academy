About Us

RHYTHM DANCE ACADEMY

Rhythm Dance Academy is a world class dance studio that spreads the love of Indian Dance focussing on one of the ancient Indian Classical Dance "Odissi". Other than Odissi we teach traditional, semi-classical, folk and creative Indian dance, with classes available both in-studio and online.Due to covid we are running our class now in online only but will start offline too very soon.

Rhythm Dance Academy offers Foundation Course for students aged 5 to 10 and Development Course for students aged 10 and above. The courses prepare emerging dance artists for a portfolio career in dance. We have a vibrant and diverse student body of around 250 students, where you are recognised as an individual and join a community of makers, leaders, educators, and performers.Our team have a deep love and respect for the life changing power of performance. As a team we’re lucky to witness great moments in the lives of young people right here in our studios. We watch them make new friends, encourage each other and mature as performers.Rhythm offers every student a safe space to discover the importance of self expression – in class, on stage and in life.
